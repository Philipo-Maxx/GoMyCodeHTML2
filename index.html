Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Fettucine Alfredo

    Cook pasta, melt butter in a saucepan, then cook with garlic.Then stir in heavy cream, cheese, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg, finally toss pasta in sauce.

  • Honey-Garlic Chicken

    Season and brown chicken, make honey-garlic sauce. Add butter to skillet, then cook,basting chicken with honey mixture, until butter is melted.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Crunchy muesli bars

    Toast oats and nuts, mix with glue (peanut butter and honey), press in pan, chill to firm, cut then bake.

  • Milk Pudding

    Mix half of milk with sugar, and half of the milk with cornstarch. Slow heat then pour in cornstarch and milk mixture. Keep stirring till it thickens, cool down and coat with coconut shreds
